Welcome

Hi, I'm sqvrltastic and I'm a digital artist. This is my main place for posting my artworks and sharing my thoughts. Welcome to my website! ðŸŽ¨

The content on this site is SFW, but it is not intended for children. Some artworks are slightly suggestive. Also, I swear, a lot occasionally. Keep that in mind.

If you want to reach me, your best bet is to shoot me an email at sqvrltastic@gmail.com; I check my email inbox regularly.

You can also sign my guestbook!

I also have a profile on GitHub and I post my art on Reddit, but I'm not as active there as I'm trying to limit my use of social media.

Commission status: closed.

I don't take commissions at the moment because, honestly, I just don't feel ready yet. For now, I am still focused on growing and developing a semi-consistent and efficient drawing process.
