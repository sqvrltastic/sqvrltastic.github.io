Art resources links dump

Updated: 01/05/2026

Content Warning for some mild artistic nudity in some of the links!

Art-related YouTube channels that I like

Proko YouTube channel — I feel like this is one of the best free resources out there for learning figure drawing.

The Art Mentor — I like his advice on art commissions and his take on chasing social media popularity.

Kristian Nee — soulful podcasts and interviews with various artists.

Artbook Addiction — another relaxing channel with some thoughtful opinions on various art-related subjects.

Color theory and lighting

Sidenote: I feel like 95% of all "color theory" videos and tutorials online are completely useless in practice. At least, they haven't helped me in the slightest in trying to understand how to paint light and pick colors. Things that actually helped me understand how light works is studying 3D rendering in Blender and some of the video listed below...

How to Choose Colours that Work! by Sycra.

The Secret to Painting the Right Color from Proko channel.

Warm Light = Cool Shadows? Or Is It A Myth? by Lighting Mentor.

Miscellaneous

PureRef — useful free little software for keeping and managing an image reference board.

Skull Sketcher v2 — small program that lets you spin and light a 3D model of a skull, useful for anatomy drawing practice.

Male head 3D reference tool and Female head 3D reference tool by William Nguyen on ArtStation — again, another super useful drawing practice tool,

Sketchfab — this is a 3D model hosting website, but it's also a great way to look for various references that you can move and twist around.

Color Hunt — just some cool palettes for inspiration.

Photoshop brushes — self-explanatory. Huge collection of free brushes.

Glaze and Nightshade — Windows-only software that adds a layer of protection to your images to prevent generative AI models from being successfully trained on them. Effectiveness is disputed.

Rebelle — digital painting software that simulates traditional medium that looks very promising, but I haven't personally tried it yet.
