Art resources links dump

Last updated: January 30th 2025

Content warning for some mild artistic nudity in some links!

Proko YouTube channel — feel like this is one of the best free resources out there for learning figure drawing

PureRef — useful free little software for keeping and managing a reference board.

Skull Sketcher v2 — small program that lets you spin and light a 3D model of a skull, useful for anatomy drawing practice.

Male head 3D reference tool and Female head 3D reference tool by William Nguyen on ArtStation — again, another super useful drawing practice tool,

Sketchfab — this is a 3D model hosting website, but it’s also a great way to look for various references that you can move and twist around.

Color Hunt — just some cool palettes for inspiration.

Websites for timed figure gesture drawing exercises:

  • Quickposes
  • SketchDaily
  • Line of Action
  • AdorkaStock

Glaze and Nightshade — Windows-only software that adds a layer of protection to your images to prevent generative AI models from being successfully trained on them.
